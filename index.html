How to evaluate a cryptocurrency

Check the technology

What consensus mechanism does it use? How fast are transactions? What's the block size? Understanding the technical architecture tells you what a blockchain can and can't do.

Evaluate the team and development

Look at GitHub activity, the founding team's track record, and the project's governance model. Active development and transparent leadership are strong positive signals.

Understand tokenomics

How many tokens exist? Is there a max supply? How are new tokens created? Inflationary vs. deflationary models have very different implications for long-term value.

Assess the ecosystem

A blockchain is only as valuable as the applications built on it. Check total value locked (TVL), number of active developers, and real user adoption — not just market cap.
